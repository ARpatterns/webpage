AR Pattern Diagrams

AR Pattern Diagrams offer a structured way to visualize the flow of AR programs through the use of Event-Condition-Action (ECA) rules. These diagrams act as a pseudo-code representation, showing the relationship between an event, a condition (if any), and the resulting action. They simplify the complexity of AR logic into an easy-to-read format, making it ideal for communication, debugging, and design.

AR Pattern Diagrams are a powerful tool for AR developers, helping you visualize and document complex event-driven logic. They provide a clear, structured way to communicate program flows, whether you're debugging or designing. Dive into the world of AR Pattern Diagrams and streamline your development process!
