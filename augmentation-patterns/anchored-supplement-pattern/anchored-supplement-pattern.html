Augmentation Pattern

Anchored Supplement Pattern

Anchored supplements provide additional information that is aligned with a detected entity. For instance, a virtual 3D information panel can be anchored beside an image detected in the real world. Similarly, a guiding sign can be anchored towards a detected door to assist with navigation.

Placement and alignment

Anchored content can be placed relative to a detected object. The content is then aligned with the object or towards the the object or user.
